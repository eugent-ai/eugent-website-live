Solution · Zeus Platform

The Zeus Platform Coming soon

The platform that commands and integrates AI agents. Still in development, and soon the foundation of your ecosystem of agents.

The Zeus Platform is the underlying AI and automation layer of Eugent on which specialised AI agents are built, commanded, managed and integrated. The platform is modular and supports the use of different AI models, agents, MCP servers, memory solutions and external systems, without depending on one specific vendor or technology.

New functionality can be added centrally and then made available to the connected agents in a controlled way, agents that keep evolving as new AI models, techniques and integrations become available.

Our vision is that in the future organisations will not use a single AI solution, but an ecosystem of specialised AI agents that work together around business processes, software development, knowledge management and operational support. Zeus forms the central foundation for this: the platform that commands these agents.

Through this platform approach, organisations stay flexible in their choice of AI models, cloud platforms and infrastructure, while investments in knowledge, configuration and integrations are reused to the fullest.
