About Eugent

Eugent builds AI agents that fit the reality of European organisations: sovereign, portable and ready for what comes next.

Eugent delivers AI agents for businesses. The agents connect to the client’s existing systems, with their own memory, their own neural network and a vendor independent choice of model. This creates agents that truly work within your processes, rather than a standalone tool next to your existing landscape.

Our conviction is that organisations do not want to be locked into a single vendor or a single model. Technology changes fast, and those who want to stay sovereign need solutions that move with it. That is why we build from open standards, portability and data ownership, with the choice to run hosted by Eugent or on premise.

Our flagship, the Zeus Platform, is still in development and will soon command an entire ecosystem of specialised agents. Until then we help organisations forward with consultancy and advice, so the step towards AI can be taken today, considered and with an eye on tomorrow.

What we stand for

  • Dedicated memory per agent
  • Vendor-independent model choice
  • Hosted by Eugent or on premise
  • Built on open standards
  • Zeus Platform in development
